VeinLine uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Compliance: We comply with healthcare data protection regulations

General eligibility requirements include:

  • Age: 18-65 years old
  • Weight: At least 50 kg (110 lbs)
  • Hemoglobin: Minimum levels (women: 12.5 g/dL, men: 13.5 g/dL)
  • Health: No serious medical conditions
  • No recent infections or vaccinations
  • No blood-borne disease risks

Use our Eligibility Checker for a preliminary assessment.
